The ctleelab was selected for an Air Force Office of Scientific Research (AFOSR) Young Investigator Program (YIP) award.
Special thanks to Cailyn Sakurai, Carolina Sarto, and Honor Akenuwa whose preliminary data were critical to the success of the proposal. 
This grant will support our work on studying how stressors influence mitochondria structure and function.
