I am an undergraduate student studying Nanotechnology at Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM) in Ensenada, Baja California. 
My research interests lie at the intersection of chemistry, biology, and nanoscale engineering, with a focus on developing innovative biomedical and molecular tools. 
I have worked on a research project involving the identification of molecular fragments to facilitate the diffusion of anticancer compounds, using computational molecular analysis. 
I am especially interested in molecular modeling, experimental design, and data-driven analysis. 
In the future, I hope to contribute to biomedical research, diagnostics, and drug discovery through nanotechnology-based approaches.
